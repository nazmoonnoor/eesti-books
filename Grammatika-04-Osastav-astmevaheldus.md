# 4 · Osastav. Noomeni astmevaheldus. Partitive Singular, Gradation of Nouns

Chapter 4 of the *Eesti keele grammatika* reference, **book pages 13–17**. How the partitive singular is formed and used, and the gradation (astmevaheldus) of nouns — quantity and quality alternation.

> Estonian first, English alongside. Word triples are **nominative : genitive : partitive** (the genitive stem is often underlined in the book; here it is the middle form).

## 1. Osastava moodustamine · Forming the partitive

The partitive singular answers the questions **keda?** *whom?*, **mida?** *what?*. It is formed by adding **-t, -d** or **-da**, or by means of the **stem vowel**.

### Osastav -t

The partitive ending is **-t** in the following cases:

**1) the word has three or more syllables in the genitive singular:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| raamat : raamatu : **raamatut** | book : of a book : book (part.) |
| maasikas : maasika : **maasikat** | strawberry : of a strawberry : strawberry (part.) |
| hele : heleda : **heledat** | bright : of bright : bright (part.) |

**2) a two-syllable word whose genitive first syllable has a long/overlong sound or a sound combination:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| valge : valge : **valget** | white : of white : white (part.) |
| roosa : roosa : **roosat** | pink : of pink : pink (part.) |

**3) the word ends in -ne or -s in the nominative:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| harjutus : harjutuse : **harjutust** | exercise : of an exercise : exercise (part.) |
| naine : naise : **naist** | woman : of a woman : woman (part.) |
| soodne : soodsa : **soodsat** | favourable : of favourable : favourable (part.) |

**4) the word ends in -l, -n or -r in the nominative:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| number : numbri : **numbrit** | number : of a number : number (part.) |
| peegel : peegli : **peeglit** | mirror : of a mirror : mirror (part.) |

**5) the nominative ends in -l, -n, -r with one syllable, and its genitive ending in -e has two syllables:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| keel : keele : **keelt** | language : of a language : language (part.) |
| noor : noore : **noort** | young : of young : young (part.) |

**6) a gradational word with the weak grade in the nominative, the strong grade in the genitive and the weak grade in the partitive** (see *Gradation* below):

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| mõte : mõtte : **mõtet** | thought : of a thought : thought (part.) |
| hammas : hamba : **hammast** | tooth : of a tooth : tooth (part.) |

### Osastav -d

The partitive ends in **-d**:

**1) after a long or overlong vowel or diphthong:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| maa : maa : **maad** | land : of land : land (part.) |
| või : või : **võid** | butter : of butter : butter (part.) |
| hea : hea : **head** | good : of good : good (part.) |
| meie, meie, **meid** | we, our ~ ours, us |

**2) in the pronouns *mind* (me) and *sind* (you).**

**3) in irregular words:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| veri : vere : **verd** | blood : of blood : blood (part.) |
| meri : mere : **merd** | sea : of sea : sea (part.) |
| lumi : lume : **lund** | snow : of snow : snow (part.) |
| uni : une : **und** | sleep : of sleep : sleep (part.) |
| mõni : mõne : **mõnd** | some : of some : some (part.) |
| tuli : tule : **tuld** | fire : of fire : fire (part.) |

In some pronouns the partitive ends in **-da**: keda *whom*, mida *what*, seda *this*, toda *that*, teda *him; her*.

### Osastav tüvevokaaliga (with the stem vowel)

**1) in two-syllable non-gradational words whose first syllable contains short sounds:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| pesa : pesa : **pesa** | nest : of a nest : nest (part.) |
| tädi : tädi : **tädi** | aunt : of an aunt : aunt (part.) |
| ema : ema : **ema** | mother : mother's : mother (part.) |
| suvi : suve : **suve** | summer : of summer : summer (part.) |

**2) in gradational words with the strong grade in the nominative, the weak grade in the genitive, and the strong grade in the partitive:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| kool : kooli : **kooli** | school : of a school : school (part.) |
| päev : päeva : **päeva** | day : of a day : day (part.) |
| kapp : kapi : **kappi** | cabinet : of a cabinet : cabinet (part.) |
| lamp : lambi : **lampi** | lamp : of a lamp : lamp (part.) |
| mägi : mäe : **mäge** | hill; mountain : of a hill : hill (part.) |
| sõda : sõja : **sõda** | war : of a war : war (part.) |
| kiri : kirja : **kirja** | letter : of a letter : letter (part.) |

## 2. Osastava kasutamine · Using the partitive

**The partitive is used:**

1. for the **object when an action is not complete but progressive** — *Õpetaja kirjutab sel suvel **õpikut**.* The teacher is writing **a textbook** this summer.
2. when an action is directed towards a **part of a thing** (uncountable material) or a phenomenon — *(partitive)* *Ema ostis lapsele **jäätist**.* Mother bought her child **some ice-cream.** · cf. *(genitive)* *Ema ostis lapsele jäätise.* Mother bought her child **an** ice-cream.
3. **numeral + the partitive singular** — *Mul on üks tütar ja **kaks poega**.* I have one daughter and **two sons**.
4. **amount + the partitive singular** — *Kui palju maksab **kilo suhkrut**?* How much does **a kilogramme of sugar** cost? · *Ma ostan **pudeli mineraalvett**.* I'll buy **a bottle of mineral water**.
5. **negation + the partitive singular** — *Täna **ei ole loengut**.* Today there is **no lecture**.

**Verbs that require the partitive:** armastama *love*, austama *respect*, eitama *deny*, hoidma *hold, keep*, huvitama *interest*, imetlema *admire*, kallistama *hug*, kartma *fear*, kiitma *praise*, kohtama *meet*, kuulama *listen*, laitma *disapprove*, mõjutama *affect*, mängima *play*, märkama *notice*, nautima *enjoy*, nägema *see*, ootama *wait*, otsima *seek*, pooldama *favour*, ravima *treat*, ründama *attack*, sallima *tolerate*, solvama *offend*, süüdistama *accuse*, taipama *realize*, taotlema *apply for*, teadma *know*, teenindama *serve*, toitma *feed*, tutvustama *introduce*, tähistama *mark, celebrate*, tänama *thank*, uurima *study*, vaatama *look*, vajama *need*, valdama *possess*, vihkama *hate*, vältima *avoid*, taga ajama *chase*, tähele panema *notice*, etc.

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Ma kuulan **raadiot** iga päev. | I listen to the **radio** every day. |
| Sinu **sõpra** huvitab sport. | Your **friend** is interested in sports. |
| Me vaatasime eile õhtul **televiisorit**. | We watched **TV** last night. |

## 3. Noomeni astmevaheldus · Gradation of Nouns

Gradation does not occur in all word stems. Many words change only their inflectional endings and markers when declined or conjugated; a clear distinction between the first three cases is made only where there are **stem changes**. To tell whether a word is gradational, compare three forms — **nominative, genitive, partitive**. Estonian nouns alternate either from the **strong grade → weak grade**, or from the **weak grade → strong grade**.

- **Strong → weak (SG : WG : SG):** nominative in the strong grade, genitive in the weak, partitive in the strong — *TA (tugev aste) = kott, NA (nõrk aste) = koti, SG = kotti* "bag".
- **Weak → strong (WG : SG : WG):** nominative in the weak grade, genitive in the strong, partitive in the weak — *mõte : mõtte : mõtet* "thought".

The **nominative and partitive always take the same grade**.

| Quantity alternation | Quality alternation — Assimilation | Quality alternation — Loss |
|---|---|---|
| **SG : WG : SG** — kapp : kapi : kappi *cabinet* · lamp : lambi : lampi *lamp* | SG: *d, b* → WG: *rr, nn, ll, mm*. **SG : WG : SG** — sild : silla : silda *bridge* | SG: *b, d, g, k, t, s* → WG: *ø, (v, j)*. **SG : WG : SG** — tuba : toa : tuba *room* · laud : laua : lauda *table* |
| **SG : WG : SG** — kool : kooli : kooli *school* · arst : arsti : arsti *doctor* | **WG : SG : WG** — lammas : lamba : lammast *sheep* | viga : vea : viga *mistake* · aeg : aja : aega *time* |
| **WG : SG : WG** — lepe : leppe : lepet *agreement* | | |

### Vältevaheldus · Quantity alternation

Quantity alternation (*vältevaheldus*) occurs if the **length** of the stem sounds changes. Many words with quantity alternation do not reveal the change in their spelling.

| SG : WG : SG | | WG : SG : WG | |
|---|---|---|---|
| piim : piima : piima | milk | pinge : pinge : pinget | tension |
| päev : päeva : päeva | day | võõras : võõra : võõrast | stranger |
| silm : silma : silma | eye | kallis : kalli : kallist | dear, expensive |

Stop consonants of some stems in the strong grade are spelt **kk, pp, tt**. In some stem patterns, stops are spelt with double letters in the strong grade and single letters in the weak grade, or with **k, p, t** in the strong grade and **g, b, d** in the weak grade:

| Muutus | Sõna | English |
|---|---|---|
| **pp : p** | sepp : sepa : seppa | smith |
| **p : b** | vaip : vaiba : vaipa | carpet |
| **tt : t** | ratas : ratta : ratast | wheel |
| **t : d** | saade : saate : saadet | broadcast |

### Laadivaheldus · Quality alternation

Quality alternation (*laadivaheldus*) occurs where stop consonants or *s* are present in the strong grade and absent in the weak grade.

**Assimilation (assimilatsioon)** — a stop consonant or *s* becomes the same sound as the preceding voiced consonant:

| Muutus | SG : WG : SG | | Muutus | WG : SG : WG | |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **nd : nn** | tund : tunni : tundi | hour | **mb : mm** | hammas : hamba : hammast | tooth |
| **rd : rr** | kord : korra : korda | order | **nd : nn** | kinnas : kinda : kinnast | glove |
| **ld : ll** | põld : põllu : põldu | field | | | |
| **rs : rr** | kõrs : kõrre : kõrt | straw | | | |

**Loss of sounds (häälikute kadu):**

1. *k, t, b, d, g, s* in the weak stem **disappear**:

| SG : WG : SG | English |
|---|---|
| luba : loa : luba | permit |
| viga : vea : viga | mistake |
| luud : luua : luuda | broom |
| koht : koha : kohta | place |
| kask : kase : kaske | birch |
| käsi : käe : kätt | hand; arm |

2. *k, t, b, d, g, s* in the weak stem are **replaced by other sounds**:

| SG : WG : SG | | WG : SG : WG | |
|---|---|---|---|
| leib : leiva : leiba | bread | varvas : varba : varvast | toe |
| sada : saja : sada | hundred | hüljes : hülge : hüljest | seal |
| poeg : poja : poega | son | | |
