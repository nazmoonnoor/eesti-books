# 3 · Nimetav ja omastav. Nominative & Genitive Singular, Nominative Plural

Chapter 3 of the *Eesti keele grammatika* reference, **book pages 10–12**. The nominative and genitive singular, the nominative plural, and a full overview table of all 14 cases.

> Estonian first, English alongside. Word triples are given as **nominative : genitive : partitive** where relevant.

## 1. Singulari nominatiiv · Nominative singular

The nominative answers the questions **kes?** *who?*, **mis?** *what?*. The nominative singular has **no case ending**.

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| See **maja** on minu oma. | **This house** is mine. |
| Mul on **ilus kodu**. | I have a **nice home**. |

---

## 2. Singulari genitiiv · Genitive singular

The genitive (possessive) answers the questions **kelle?** *whose?*, **mille?** *of what?*. The genitive singular **always ends in a vowel**. Where the nominative singular ends in a vowel, the genitive usually keeps the same vowel. The genitive is the **base form** for all the singular cases from the illative onward.

| Nimetav | | Omastav | |
|---|---|---|---|
| onu | uncle | onu | uncle's |
| valge | white | valge | of white |
| öö | night | öö | of a night |
| süü | fault, guilt | süü | of guilt |
| kivi | stone | kivi | of a stone |

Where the nominative ends in a **consonant**, the genitive ends in **-a, -e, -i, -u**:

| Nimetav | | Omastav | |
|---|---|---|---|
| haigus | illness, disease | haiguse | of an illness |
| päev | day | päeva | of a day |
| eksam | exam | eksami | of an exam |
| õpik | textbook | õpiku | of a textbook |

**Exceptions (erandid):**

| Nimetav | | Omastav | | Reegel |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| sinine | blue | sinise | of blue | nominatiiv **-ne** → genitiiv **-se** |
| teater | theatre | teatri | of a theatre | **-er, -el** → **-ri, -li** |
| kahvel | fork | kahvli | of a fork | |
| sõber | friend | sõbra | friend's, of a friend | **-er** → **-ra** |
| töökas | hard-working | tööka | of hard-working | **-kas** → **-ka** |

More consonant-change patterns:

| Nimetav | | Omastav | | Muutus |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| saabas | boot | saapa | of a boot | **-as** → **-a** |
| suvi | summer | suve | of summer | **-i** → **-e** |
| habe | beard | habeme | of a beard | **-e** → **-eme** |
| hale | sad | haleda | of sad | **-e** → **-eda** |

**The genitive is used for:**

1. the **attribute** preceding the head-word, to indicate possession — *See on **sõbra** auto.* This is my **friend's** car. · *Osta mulle **eesti keele** õpik.* Buy me a textbook **of Estonian**.
2. the **object** in the case of a completed action — *Õpetaja kirjutab **õpiku**.* The teacher will write **a textbook**.
3. the **object** when the action is directed at the object as a whole — *Ma võtan oma **koti**.* I'll take my **bag**.
4. the **adjectival attribute** in the last four cases when the attribute does not agree with the head-word — *Mees läks **suure kivini** ja pööras paremale.* The man went **as far as the big rock** and turned right.

## 3. Pluurali nominatiiv · Nominative plural

The nominative plural answers **kes?** *who?*, **mis?** *what?*. It is formed from the **genitive singular stem** by adding the plural marker **-d**.

| nimetav : omastav : osastav > mitmuse nimetav | English |
|---|---|
| tänav : tänava : tänavat > tänava**d** | street : of a street : street (part.) > streets |
| naaber : naabri : naabrit > naabri**d** | neighbour : neighbour's : neighbour (part.) > neighbours |
| kapp : kapi : kappi > kapi**d** | cabinet : of a cabinet : cabinet (part.) > cabinets |

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Naine läks turule. | A woman went to the market. |
| **Naised** läksid turule. | **Women** went to the market. |
| Sel aastal oli suvi soe. | This year the summer was warm. |
| Eestis on **suved** tavaliselt jahedad. | In Estonia **summers** are usually cool. |

## 4. Käänete tabel · The cases at a glance

All 14 Estonian cases, shown with *ilus koht* ("beautiful place") in singular and plural.

| Kääne (Case) | Küsimus (Question) | Ainsus (Singular) | Mitmus (Plural) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Nimetav (Nominative) | kes? mis? | ilus koht | ilusad kohad |
| Omastav (Genitive) | kelle? mille? | ilusa koha | ilusate kohtade |
| Osastav (Partitive) | keda? mida? | ilusa**t** koht**a** | ilusa**id** koht**i** ~ kohta**sid** |
| Sisseütlev (Illative) | kellesse? millesse? kuhu? | ilusa**sse** koht**a** ~ koha**sse** | ilusa**tesse** kohta**desse** |
| Seesütlev (Inessive) | kelles? milles? kus? | ilusa**s** koha**s** | ilusa**tes** kohta**des** |
| Seestütlev (Elative) | kellest? millest? kust? | ilusa**st** koha**st** | ilusa**test** kohta**dest** |
| Alaleütlev (Allative) | kellele? millele? kuhu? | ilusa**le** koha**le** | ilusa**tele** kohta**dele** |
| Alalütlev (Adessive) | kellel? millel? kus? | ilusa**l** koha**l** | ilusa**tel** kohta**del** |
| Alaltütlev (Ablative) | kellelt? millelt? kust? | ilusa**lt** koha**lt** | ilusa**telt** kohta**delt** |
| Saav (Translative) | kelleks? milleks? | ilusa**ks** koha**ks** | ilusa**teks** kohta**deks** |
| Rajav (Terminative) | kelleni? milleni? | ilusa koha**ni** | ilusate kohta**deni** |
| Olev (Essive) | kellena? millena? | ilusa koha**na** | ilusate kohta**dena** |
| Ilmaütlev (Abessive) | kelleta? milleta? | ilusa koha**ta** | ilusate kohta**deta** |
| Kaasaütlev (Comitative) | kellega? millega? | ilusa koha**ga** | ilusate kohta**dega** |
