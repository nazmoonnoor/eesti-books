## 2. Tuul ja ilmastik
*(pages 72–74 — wind directions and weather words)*

**Tuul *(wind)* — "Puhub … tuul."**

| Suund | Tuul | English |
|---|---|---|
| põhi (N) | põhjatuul | north wind |
| kirre (NE) | kirdetuul | north-east wind |
| ida (E) | idatuul | east wind |
| kagu (SE) | kagutuul | south-east wind |
| lõuna (S) | lõunatuul | south wind |
| edel (SW) | edelatuul | south-west wind |
| lääs (W) | läänetuul | west wind |
| loe (NW) | loodetuul | north-west wind |

**Ilmastik *(weather)*:**

| Estonian | English | Estonian | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| päike | sun | torm | storm |
| pilves | cloudy | äike | thunderstorm |
| selge | clear | välk | lightning |
| vihm | rain | udu | fog |
| lumi | snow | sula | thaw |
| lörts | sleet | taevas | sky |
| rahe | hail | kraad | degree |

> 🔹 Verbs: **puhub** *(blows)* · **paistab** *(shines)* · **sajab** *(it's raining/snowing)* · **on pilves** *(is cloudy)*.

## 3. Ilmateade
*(pages 74–75 — make and report a forecast)*

**Homme on … ilm:** *Vihma … . Puhub … tuul … kuni … meetrit sekundis. … Eestis on … . Õhutemperatuur on … .*

**Telefonis *(on the phone)*:**

– Hallo! Kus sa oled?<br>
– Oi, kui tore! Kuidas ilm on?<br>
– Kui palju sooja on?<br>
– Kas tuult on?<br>
– Kas vihma / lörtsi / lund / rahet sajab?<br>
– Nii tore / õudne! Tule ruttu koju!

<details class="tr">
<summary>🇬🇧 Translation</summary>

*– Hello! Where are you? – Oh, how nice! What's the weather like? – How warm is it? – Is it windy? – Is it raining / sleeting / snowing / hailing? – So nice / awful! Come home quickly!*

</details>
