## 1. Impersonaal · Impersonal Passive

The impersonal passive is used when the **agent** of the action is **not mentioned** in a sentence. The impersonal forms are formed from the **-tud participle** stem (see [Chapter 18: Participles](Grammatika-18-Partitsiibid)).

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Teises toas **lauldakse**. | Someone is singing in the next room. |
| Teises toas **lauldi**. | Someone was singing in the next room. |

The impersonal can only be used when the agent is a **living being**. The impersonal **cannot** be used with verbs that in the personal usually occur only in the 3rd person (sadama *rain*, helendama *glow*).

---

## 2. Impersonaali preesens e umbisikulise tegumoe olevik · Present Impersonal

The affirmative present indicative impersonal is formed from the **-tud participle** by adding the marker **-ta/kse, -da/kse,** or **-a/kse**. There are **no personal endings**.

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| lugema, lugeda, loen, loetud > loeta**kse** | to read > is read |
| õmblema, õmmelda, õmmeldud > õmmelda**kse** | to sew > is sewn |
| käima, käia, käin, käidud > käia**kse** | to go, to walk > (people) go, walk |

The marker of the negative present is **-ta** or **-da**.

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| saatma, saata, saadan, saadetud > ei saade**ta** | to send > is not sent |
| laulma, laulda, laulan, lauldud > ei laul**da** | to sing > is not sung |

The choice of the impersonal marker depends on whether the -tud participle marker is **-tud** or **-dud**.

Generally, words with the **-tud** marker take the impersonal markers **-takse, -ta**.

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| hakkama, hakata, hakatud > hakata**kse**, ei haka**ta** | to begin > is begun, is not begun |

Word stems ending in **l, n, r** and with the participle marker **-dud** take the impersonal markers **-dakse, -da**.

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| laulma, laulda, lauldud > laulda**kse**, ei laul**da** | is sung, is not sung |
| naerma, naerda, naerdud > naerda**kse**, ei naer**da** | (people) laugh, (people) don't laugh |

Irregular verbs with stem changes, such as **olema** *be* and **sööma** *eat*, add the present impersonal marker **-akse** to the -da infinitive stem. The negative present impersonal marker is **-da**, which is added to the -tud participle stem.

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| sööma, süüa, söön, söödud > süüa**kse**, ei söö**da** | is eaten, is not eaten |
| olema, olla, olen, oldud > olla**kse**, ei ol**da** | (someone) is, is not |

**Verbs with irregular impersonal forms:**

| -ma infinitive | -da infinitive | Affirmative present | -tud participle | Negative present |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| sööma *to eat* | süüa | süüa/kse | söö/dud | ei söö/da |
| lööma *to hit* | lüüa | lüüa/kse | löö/dud | ei löö/da |
| viima *to take away* | viia | viia/kse | vii/dud | ei vii/da |
| tooma *to bring* | tuua | tuua/kse | too/dud | ei too/da |
| jooma *to drink* | juua | juua/kse | joo/dud | ei joo/da |
| looma *to create* | luua | luua/kse | loo/dud | ei loo/da |
| käima *to walk* | käia | käia/kse | käi/dud | ei käi/da |
| olema *to be* | olla | oll/akse | ol/dud | ei ol/da |
| tulema *to come* | tulla | tull/akse | tul/dud | ei tul/da |
| surema *to die* | surra | surr/akse | sur/dud | ei sur/da |
| panema *to put* | panna | pann/akse | pan/dud | ei pan/da |
| minema *to go* | minna | minn/akse | min/dud | ei min/da |

Some irregular verbs that add **-ta** in the negative present form:

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| tegema, teha, teen, tehtud > teh**akse**, ei teh**ta** | is done, is not done |
| nägema, näha, näen, nähtud > näh**akse**, ei näh**ta** | is seen, is not seen |

*(See -tud participle, [Chapter 18](Grammatika-18-Partitsiibid).)*

## 3. Impersonaali imperfekt e umbisikulise tegumoe lihtminevik · Imperfect Impersonal

The imperfect marker is **-ti** or **-di**, which is generally added to the **-tud participle** stem.

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| küsima, küsida, küsin, küsitud > küsi**ti** | asked, was asked |
| kuulma, kuulda, kuulen, kuuldud > kuul**di** | heard, was heard |
| käima, käia, käin, käidud > käi**di** | went, people went |

The negative imperfect consists of **ei + -tud participle**.

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Teises toas **ei lauldud**. | No one sang in the next room. |

**Non-gradational verbs:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| kirjutama, kirjutada, kirjutatud > kirjuta**takse**, kirjuta**ti** | is written, was written |

**Gradational verbs (SG : WG : SG):**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| hakkama, hakata, hakkan, hakatud > hakata**kse**, haka**ti** | is begun, was begun |
| õmblema, õmmelda, õmblen, õmmeldud > õmmelda**kse**, õmmel**di** | is sewn, was sewn |

**Gradational verbs (SG : SG : WG):**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| õppima, õppida, õpin, õpitud > õpita**kse**, õpi**ti** | is studied, was studied |
| lugema, lugeda, loen, loetud > loeta**kse**, loe**ti** | is read, was read |

1. **-ma is preceded by -d:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| kandma, kanda, kannan, kantud > kanta**kse**, kan**ti** | is carried, was carried |

Similarly: andma *to give*, hoidma *to hold*, jõudma *to reach*, leidma *to find*, püüdma *to catch*, teadma *to know*.

2. **-ma is preceded by -t:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| sõitma, sõita, sõidan, sõidetud > sõideta**kse**, sõide**ti** | is driven, was driven |

Similarly: kartma *to fear*, keetma *to boil*, kiitma *to praise*, lootma *to hope*, nutma *to cry*, ostma *to buy*, saatma *to send*, tahtma *to want*, tootma *to produce*, tõstma *to lift*, võitma *to win*, väitma *to claim*.

**Exceptions:**

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| ajama, ajada, ajan, aetud > aeta**kse**, ae**ti** | is driven, was driven |
| jätma, jätta, jätan, jäetud > jäeta**kse**, jäe**ti** | is left, was left |
| võtma, võtta, võtan, võetud > võeta**kse**, võe**ti** | is taken, was taken |
| kütma, kütta, kütan, köetud > köeta**kse**, köe**ti** | is heated, was heated |
| matma, matta, matan, maetud > maeta**kse**, mae**ti** | is buried, was buried |

*(See -tud participle, [Chapter 18](Grammatika-18-Partitsiibid).)*

## 4. Impersonaali perfekt e umbisikulise tegumoe täisminevik · Perfect Impersonal

The perfect form consists of the present form of the verb **olema** and the **-tud participle** of the main verb.

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| andma, anda, annan, antud > **on** antud | has been given |
| õppima, õppida, õpin, õpitud > **on** õpitud | has been studied |
| tegema, teha, teen, tehtud > **on** tehtud | has been done |
| naerma, naerda, naeran, naerdud > **on** naerdud | has been laughed (at) |

The negative form of the perfect impersonal consists of **ei + ole + -tud** participle.

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Toit **ei ole** veel **söödud**. | The food hasn't been eaten yet. |

---

## 5. Impersonaali pluskvamperfekt e umbisikulise tegumoe enneminevik · Pluperfect Impersonal

The pluperfect impersonal consists of the **imperfect form** of the verb **olema** and the **-tud participle** of the main verb.

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| sööma, süüa, söön, söödud > **oli** söödud | had been eaten |
| õmblema, õmmelda, õmblen, õmmeldud > **oli** õmmeldud | had been sewn |
| hüppama, hüpata, hüppan, hüpatud > **oli** hüpatud | had been jumped |

The negative form of the pluperfect impersonal consists of **ei + olnud + -tud** participle.

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Töö **ei olnud** eilseks veel **lõpetatud**. | The work hadn't been finished yet by yesterday. |

## 7. Objektikääne ja isikuline/umbisikuline tegumood · Object Case and Personal/Impersonal

**Object Case (sg) and Personal / Impersonal:**

| Affirmative form of the indicative personal + genitive/partitive | Affirmative form of the indicative impersonal + nominative/partitive |
|---|---|
| Ma avan **ukse**. (genitive) *I'll open the door.* | **Uks** avatakse. (nominative) *The door is opened.* |
| Ma kuulasin **muusikat**. (partitive) *I was listening to the music.* | Toas kuulatakse **muusikat**. (partitive) *People are listening to the music in the room.* |

| Negative form + partitive | Negative form + partitive |
|---|---|
| Ma ei kirjutanud **harjutust**. (partitive) *I wasn't doing a written exercise.* | Tunnis ei kirjutatud **harjutust**. (partitive) *The written exercise was not done in the classroom.* |

The plural forms of the object cases are discussed in [Partitive Plural, Chapter 8](Grammatika-08-Mitmuse-osastav).
