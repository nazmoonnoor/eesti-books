8. Toa kirjeldus

(page 34 — describe a room: read and draw)

Toa keskel on väike voodi.
Akna all on jalgratas.
Ukse juures on nagi.
Nurgas on suur kass.
Põrandal on ilus vaip.
Seinal on vana foto.
Laes on moodne lamp.

🇬🇧 Translation

In the middle of the room is a small bed. Under the window is a bicycle. By the door is a coat rack. In the corner is a big cat. On the floor is a nice rug. On the wall is an old photo. On the ceiling is a modern lamp.

🔹 Asukohad: toa keskel (in the middle) · nurgas (in the corner) · põrandal (on the floor) · seinal (on the wall) · laes (on the ceiling) · akna all (under the window) · ukse juures (by the door).
