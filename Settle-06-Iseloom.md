Virgo is optimistic and emotional, a real "dancing lion"; a film director. Libra is conservative and quiet, reads the news with a broad outlook; an analyst. Scorpio is restless and brave, loves cycling, travel and exotic lands; a tour guide. Sagittarius is unusual and charismatic, an extravagant "artist of life"; an artist. Capricorn is brave and temperamental, loves football and the sauna, daredevil; a stuntman. Aquarius is clever and hardworking, speaks several languages and fixes things ("golden hands"); a masseur. Pisces is polite and friendly, loves walking in the park and classical music; a musician.*
