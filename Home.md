# Nagu Eesti — Estonian A2 Study Wiki

A chapter-by-chapter learning wiki built from the **E nagu Eesti** coursebook, aimed at the **Estonian A2 language exam**.

Each chapter page follows the **same order as the book pages** and is broken into clear sections: phrases, dialogues, vocabulary, reading, grammar/revision, questions, and exercises (with answers).

## How to use this wiki

- Work through one chapter at a time, top to bottom — the order matches the printed book.
- **Estonian** is given first, with **English** in italics or in a second column.
- Try every exercise *before* opening the **Answers** drop-down.
- Use the **Word list** at the end of each chapter for quick revision.
