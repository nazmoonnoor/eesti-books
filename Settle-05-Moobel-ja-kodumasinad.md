## 6. Kus on asi?
*(pages 31 & 33 — postpositions of place; the thing takes the II vorm/genitive)*

| Postposition | English | Näide |
|---|---|---|
| all | under | õhupall on tugitooli **all** |
| peal | on (top of) | õhupall on tugitooli **peal** |
| ees | in front of | õhupall on tugitooli **ees** |
| taga | behind | õhupall on tugitooli **taga** |
| kõrval | beside | õhupall on tugitooli **kõrval** |
| kohal | above | õhupall on tugitooli **kohal** |
| vahel | between | õhupall on tugitooli ja lambi **vahel** |

> 🔹 *Kus on liblikas?* → **Liblikas on tugitooli all.** *(The butterfly is under the armchair.)* — practise with every piece of furniture.

## 8. Toa kirjeldus
*(page 34 — describe a room: read and draw)*

> Toa keskel on väike voodi.<br>
> Akna all on jalgratas.<br>
> Ukse juures on nagi.<br>
> Nurgas on suur kass.<br>
> Põrandal on ilus vaip.<br>
> Seinal on vana foto.<br>
> Laes on moodne lamp.

<details class="tr">
<summary>🇬🇧 Translation</summary>

*In the middle of the room is a small bed. Under the window is a bicycle. By the door is a coat rack. In the corner is a big cat. On the floor is a nice rug. On the wall is an old photo. On the ceiling is a modern lamp.*

</details>

> 🔹 **Asukohad:** toa keskel *(in the middle)* · nurgas *(in the corner)* · põrandal *(on the floor)* · seinal *(on the wall)* · laes *(on the ceiling)* · akna all *(under the window)* · ukse juures *(by the door)*.
