## 1. Riidepoes
*(page 99 — at the clothes shop; listen and write)*

**Mis see on?** → See on … . · **Mis need on?** → Need on … .

**Dialoog 1**

– Müüja: Tere päevast! Kas ma saan teid aidata?<br>
– Klient: Jah. … ?<br>
– Müüja: Tulge, ma näitan!<br>
– Klient: Tänan. … ?<br>
– Müüja: See maksab 40 eurot.<br>
– Klient: … ?<br>
– Müüja: Aga palun!<br>
– Klient: Oh, ilus! … .<br>
– Müüja: Väga hea! Lähme kassasse!

**Dialoog 2**

– Müüja: Tere, kas ma saan teid aidata?<br>
– Klient: Jah. … ?<br>
– Müüja: See on soodushinnaga. See kleit maksab 25 eurot.<br>
– Klient: Tore! Kas ma võin proovida?<br>
– Müüja: Muidugi! Proovikabiinid on seal.<br>
– Klient: … . — Müüja: Kuidas sobis?<br>
– Klient: See on liiga suur / väike.<br>
– Müüja: Vaatame, äkki on siin suurem number. Ongi! Proovige seda!<br>
– Müüja: Tore! Lähme kassasse! Kas soovite kilekotti ka osta?

<details class="tr">
<summary>🇬🇧 Translation</summary>

*– Hello! Can I help you? – Yes … ? – Come, I'll show you! – Thanks … ? – It costs 40 euros. – …? – Here you go! – Oh, lovely! – Great, let's go to the till!*<br>
*– Can I help you? – Yes … ? – It's on sale. This dress is 25 euros. – Great! May I try it on? – Of course! The fitting rooms are over there. – … How does it fit? – It's too big / small. – Let's see, maybe there's a bigger size here. There is! Try this one! – Great! Let's go to the till! Would you like to buy a bag too?*

</details>

## 2. Kasulikud väljendid
*(useful phrases in the shop)*

| Estonian | English |
|---|---|
| Kas ma saan teid aidata? | Can I help you? |
| Kas ma võin proovida? | May I try it on? |
| Kui palju see maksab? | How much does it cost? |
| See on soodushinnaga. | It's on sale. |
| Proovikabiinid on seal. | The fitting rooms are over there. |
| Kuidas (see) sobib? | How does it fit? |
| See on liiga suur / väike. | It's too big / small. |
| Kas teil on suurem / väiksem number? | Do you have a bigger / smaller size? |
| Lähme kassasse! | Let's go to the till! |
| Kas soovite kilekotti? | Would you like a bag? |

> 🔹 **Kirjuta!** Sort the items into **naisteriiete pood** *(women's)* · **meesteriiete pood** *(men's)* · **kingapood** *(shoe shop)*.
