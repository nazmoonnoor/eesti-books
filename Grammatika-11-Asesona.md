# 11 · Pronoomen. Asesõna. Pronouns

Chapter 11 of the *Eesti keele grammatika* reference, **book pages 37–38**. The six pronoun types: personal, possessive, reflexive, demonstrative, interrogative/relative, and reciprocal.

## 1. Personaalpronoomen e isikuline asesõna · Personal Pronouns

Personal pronouns have a **short** and a **long** form in Estonian. The long form is used to **emphasize** the pronoun. Personal pronouns can be declined in **all the cases**.

| Nominative | Genitive | Partitive | Adessive |
|---|---|---|---|
| ma ~ mina *I* | mu ~ minu *my, mine* | mind *me* | mul ~ minul *I (have)* |
| sa ~ sina *you* | su ~ sinu *your, yours* | sind *you* | sul ~ sinul *you (have)* |
| ta ~ tema *he, she, it* | ta ~ tema *his, her, hers, it, its* | teda *him, her, it* | tal ~ temal *he, she, it (has)* |
| me ~ meie *we* | me ~ meie *our, ours* | meid *us* | meil *we (have)* |
| te ~ teie *you* | te ~ teie *your, yours* | teid *you* | teil *you (have)* |
| nad ~ nemad *they* | nende *their, theirs* | neid *them* | neil ~ nendel *they (have)* |

Personal pronouns can often be **omitted**, because the person is indicated by the personal ending of the verb.

If the personal pronoun functions as an **attribute**, it does **not** take the same case as the following noun, and remains in the **genitive**.

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| See on **minu** raamat. | This is **my** book. |
| Lugesin seda **sinu** kirjast. | I read it in **your** letter. |

---

## 2. Possessiivpronoomen e omastav asesõna · Possessive Pronouns

The possessive pronoun **oma** indicates belonging to somebody or something. Usually the pronoun *oma* is **not declined** and remains in the genitive.

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Ma tunnen **oma** venna iseloomu. | I know my brother's character. |
| Ta elab **oma** uues majas juba mitu kuud. | He has lived in his new house already for several months. |

---

## 3. Refleksiivpronoomen e enesekohane asesõna · Reflexive Pronouns

Reflexive pronouns indicate that the action is directed towards the agent himself/herself. The pronoun **iseenese** (*self*) lacks the nominative case form.

| Vorm | Case | English |
|---|---|---|
| Nom. | ise | self |
| Gen. | enese ~ enda | — |
| Part. | ennast ~ end | — |
| Gen. | iseenese ~ iseenda | — |
| Part. | iseennast ~ iseend | — |

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Tütarlaps imetles **end/ennast** peeglist. | The girl admired **herself** in the mirror. |

---

## 4. Demonstratiivpronoomen e näitav asesõna · Demonstrative Pronouns

Demonstrative pronouns indicate some thing, action, or feature:

| Vorm | English |
|---|---|
| Nom. see *this*, Gen. selle, Part. seda, Illat. sellesse | this |
| Nom. need *these*, Gen. nende, Part. neid, Illat. nendesse/neisse | these |
| Nom. niisugune *such*, Gen. niisuguse, Part. niisugust, Illat. niisugusesse | such (a kind) |
| Nom. selline *such*, Gen. sellise, Part. sellist, Illat. sellisesse | such (a kind) |

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| **See** loeng meeldis mulle väga. | I liked **this** lecture very much. |
| **Need** inimesed on meie sõbrad. | **These** people are our friends. |
| **Niisugune/selline** käitumine on ebaviisakas. | **This kind of** behaviour is impolite. |

---

## 5. Interrogatiiv- ja relatiivpronoomenid e küsiv-siduvad asesõnad · Interrogative and Relative Pronouns

Pronouns used at the beginning of a question and for linking clauses of compound sentences:

| Vorm | English |
|---|---|
| Nom. mis *what*, Gen. mille, Part. mida, Illat. millesse | what |
| Nom. missugune *which*, Gen. missuguse, Part. missugust, Illat. missugusesse | which |
| Nom. kumb *which / which one*, Gen. kumma, Part. kumba, Illat. kummasse/kumba etc | which (of two) |
| Nom. kes *who*, Gen. kelle, Part. keda, Illat. kellesse | who |

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| **Kes** see on? Ma tean, **kes** see on. | **Who** is this? I know **who** it is. |

---

## 6. Retsiprookpronoomenid e vastastikused asesõnad · Reciprocal Pronouns

Reciprocal pronouns indicate reciprocal action between two or more participants.

| Vorm | English |
|---|---|
| Gen. teineteise, Part. teineteist, Illat. teineteisesse | each other |
| Gen. üksteise, Part. üksteist, Illat. üksteisesse | one another |

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| Armastajatel oli **teineteisele** palju öelda. | The lovers had much to say to **each other**. |
| Meie perekonnas peavad kõik **üksteisest** lugu. | In our family everyone respects **one another**. |
