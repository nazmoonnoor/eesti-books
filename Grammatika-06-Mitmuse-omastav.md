# 6 · Mitmuse omastav. Käänetevahelised seosed. Genitive Plural. Relations between the Cases

Chapter 6 of the *Eesti keele grammatika* reference, **book pages 23–24**. How the genitive plural is formed from the partitive singular stem, and how the rest of the plural case forms relate to it.

> Estonian first, English alongside. Word forms are **nominative : genitive : partitive > case form**.

**On this page:** [Mitmuse omastav](#1-mitmuse-omastav--genitive-plural) · [Käänetevahelised seosed](#2-käänetevahelised-seosed--relations-between-the-cases)

## 1. Mitmuse omastav · Genitive Plural

The genitive plural is formed from the **partitive singular** stem.

**Where the partitive singular ends in a vowel**, the genitive plural ends in **-de**:

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| maja : maja : maja > maja**de** | of houses |
| poeg : poja : poega > poega**de** | of sons |

**Where the partitive singular ends in -d**, the genitive plural usually ends in **-de**:

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| maa : maa : maad > maa**de** | of lands |

**Where the partitive singular ends in -t**, the genitive plural usually ends in **-te**, sometimes in **-de**:

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| aasta : aasta : aastat > aasta**te** | of years |
| raamat : raamatu : raamatut > raamatu**te** | of books |
| keel : keele : keelt > kee**lte** | of languages |
| saabas : saapa : saabast > saabas**te** | of boots |
| auto : auto : autot > auto**de** | of cars |
| pere : pere : peret > pere**de** | of families |
| tütar : tütre : tütart > tütar**de** | of daughters |

**There are a few exceptions:**

1. The genitive plural is formed from the **genitive singular** in the word *habe* 'beard' (and similarly in some other words):

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| habe : habeme : habet > habe**mete** | of beards |
| liige : liikme : liiget > liik**mete** | of members |
| võti : võtme : võtit > võt**mete** | of keys |

2. If the partitive singular ends in **-kku**, the genitive plural also has a short form:

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| kirjanik : kirjaniku : kirjanikku > kirjanik**kude** ~ kirjani**ke** | of writers |

3. Words ending in a vowel in the partitive singular take **-te** in the genitive plural:

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| poiss : poisi : poissi > pois**te** | of boys |
| nael : naela : naela > nael**te** | of nails |
| õun : õuna : õuna > õun**te** | of apples |
| koer : koera : koera > koer**te** | of dogs |

## 2. Käänetevahelised seosed · Relations between the Cases

The singular and plural cases (except for the short form of the illative singular) have the **same endings** starting from the illative.

**The nominative plural** is formed from the **genitive singular** stem by adding the plural marker **-d**:

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| rida : rea : rida > rea**d** | lines; rows |
| hammas : hamba : hammast > hamba**d** | teeth |

**The genitive plural** is formed from the **partitive singular** stem with the markers **-de** or **-te**:

| Sõna | English |
|---|---|
| tuba : toa : tuba > toa**de** | of rooms |
| hammas : hamba : hammast > hamma**ste** | of teeth |

All the remaining plural cases (except for the partitive) are formed by adding the respective case ending to the **genitive plural** form:

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| noorte tütarde > noorte**sse** tütarde**sse** : noorte**l** tütarde**l** | of young daughters > young daughters (illative) : young daughters (adessive) |

The attribute does **not** agree with the headword in the last four cases:

| Eesti keeles | In English |
|---|---|
| noor sõber, noore sõbra, noorel sõbral | young friend |
| but: noore sõbra**ni**, noore sõbra**na**, noorte sõprade**ta**, noorte sõprade**ga**. | *(comparative-style unchanging attribute)* |

The **-de** plural can be formed from all nouns.

**Käänetevahelised seosed I · Relations between the Cases I** — *nominative: hammas "tooth"*

| Kääne | Ainsus (Singular) | Mitmus (Plural) |
|---|---|---|
| Nominative | hammas | hamba+d |
| Genitive | hamba | hammas+te |
| Partitive | hammast | hamba+id |
| Illative | hamba+sse | hammaste+sse |
| Inessive | hamba+s | hammaste+s |
| Elative | hamba+st | hammaste+st |

**Käänetevahelised seosed II · Relations between the Cases II** — *nominative: jalg "leg; foot"*

| Kääne | Ainsus (Singular) | Mitmus (Plural) |
|---|---|---|
| Nominative | jalg | jalad |
| Genitive | jala | jalgade |
| Partitive | jalga | jalgu / jalgasid |
| Illative | jalga / jalasse | jalgadesse |
| Inessive | jalas | jalgades |
| Elative | jalast | jalgadest |
